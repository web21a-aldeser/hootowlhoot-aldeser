Dino Explosion

Rules:

Each player must play one of his cards on his turn, moving to the available position of the card color he chose to play. If a player has a meteor card, he is required to play it and advance the meteor token on that turn. At the end of his turn, the player must take a new card from the pile of cards and send the card he just used to the discard pile. If all the dinosaurs reached the cave before the meteorite token reached the ground, then all players win; but if there is still at least one dinosaur to enter the cave and the meteorite token reaches its peak, the game is over and the players lose.
